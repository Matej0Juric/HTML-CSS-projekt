Roland was skeptical, but the sorceress was insistent, and she offered to train him in the ways of magic.

Roland accepted her offer, and soon found himself thrust into a world of danger and adventure. He and the other Heroes, a ragtag group of warriors, wizards, and rogues, traveled across the kingdom, battling monsters and uncovering ancient artifacts. They gained strength and wisdom as they went, and before long, they were ready to face the greatest challenge of all: King Gryphonheart himself. The final battle was fierce, and many of the Heroes fell. But in the end, Roland and his companions emerged victorious. They deposed the tyrant king and restored peace to the kingdom. And as they rode off into the sunset, the people hailed them as heroes, and their legend lived on for centuries to come.
